How to Value a Business

Our most frequently asked question - “How do I value my business?”

There are many business valuation methods you can use to find out how much a company is worth. Most commonly, you would value a business based on its turnover or net profit. Valuing a company based on net profit also involves you adjusting your figures by adding back any exceptional costs that the business has incurred. You would then use a market multiple and take into account any assets and liabilities to calculate the business’s worth Working out the value of a business can be a complicated process, and getting an accurate company valuation is key to balancing maximum value versus a quick business sale. When you want to value a business for sale, you should speak with Intelligent, the professionals, to make sure you’re getting the best possible price for your business.
