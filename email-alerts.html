What are email alerts?
Creating email alerts will ensure that you will never miss the business you want. Select the trade you wish to be alerted on and you will be contacted via email when a new business is for sale under your selected trade. Additionally by using email alerts, this will save you the time from searching for new businesses every day.
How can I cancel my email alerts?
Email alerts can be cancelled at any time. By pressing delete on your selected trade alert will remove you from the alert list on that particular trade.
How long will the email alerts last for?
All alerts will remain active for a period of six months.
